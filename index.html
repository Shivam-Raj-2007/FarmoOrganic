About FarmoOrganic

We aspire to provide smart farming methods in our fields and take a step towards the new generation of farming, together. We would like to introduce our new technique which will completely analyse the soil of our fields, thus, providing immense convenience to our farmers. With the help of it, we can accurately make out the mineral content and moisture of the soil, thereby giving the farmer a pretty good idea about its fertility. We firmly believe that it will be revolutionary step towards farming, the major occupation of India.

We are four tech-savvy students studying at Delhi Public School, Patna. We come from a variety of backgrounds and we are here together to facilitate organic agriculture.
